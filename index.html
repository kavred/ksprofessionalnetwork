Why KSPN?

A Zero-Cost Vetting Intermediary

We eliminate the friction, risks, and administrative overhead of traditional student hiring. Pearland businesses gain tailored capacity for key projects, while driven students receive real-world work opportunities.

Rigorous Student Vetting

We do more than forward resumes. Our internal Student Development team pre-screens and interviews all applicants, matching only the top high school candidates with your business.

Strictly Zero-Cost

Step-by-step

Your Journey with KSPN

Partnering with us requires minimal time and zero commitments. Here is exactly what happens when you tap into our local high school talent network.

0

In-Person Scouting

Our student leadership teams proactively approach scouted businesses in person to establish connections, pitch the initiative, and secure potential internship opportunities.

1

The 30-Second Connect

Fill out our short interest form. It takes less than a minute and requires no immediate commitment. We just need to know who you are, what your industry is, and the best way to contact you.

2

The Custom Consultation

Within 5 business days, a dedicated KSPN Account Manager reaches out. We'll work with you to understand your exact project needs—whether it's an ongoing intern, a social media designer, or a job shadow.

3

The Talent Match

Sit back while we do the heavy lifting. We tap into our career clubs at Dawson and Shadow Creek High Schools, filter applications, review profiles, and select top pre-vetted candidates matching your parameters.

4

Placement & Support

We present the top resumes for your approval. You make the final hiring call. Once the student begins, your Account Manager provides structured progress checks to ensure the engagement is successful.

Tailored Formats

Flexible Support Structures

We facilitate various structural arrangements, allowing your business to structure the engagement around your specific projects and timeline.

Project-Based Work

Perfect for discrete tasks like building a landing page, generating social media content calendars, or organizing inventory logs. Students work independently to hit defined project milestones.

Structured Internships

Defined multi-week arrangements (summer or semester-based) that give students a comprehensive view of your company. Can be paid or unpaid depending on duties and hours.

Part-Time Support

Ongoing support for operations, admin, customer support, or digital marketing. Tailored to align around high school schedules (after school, weekends, and holidays).

Apprenticeships

Hands-on operational training where students master technical or vocational skills under your team's direct mentoring and oversight.

Job Shadowing

Low-friction, short-term arrangements (usually 1-3 days) where students shadow your professionals to learn about career pathways and day-to-day operations.

Volunteer Roles

Community-centered project roles for non-profits or community organizations, helping students build service hours while helping your operation grow.
